Why Carver?

Carver is committed to American jobs. Not only do they manufacture all of their boat covers at their state-of-the-art facility in Landrum, South Carolina, but almost 90% of their components and materials are also manufactured right here in the USA, while their competitors have increasingly moved manufacturing offshore. Dedicated to fit, quality, service, and American jobs for over 30 years, Carver Industries is not only an industry leader, but a brand you can trust.

Carver Industries offers the largest selection of fabrics with 7 types in 36 colors. All fabrics are crafted to be breathable for the well-being of your boat and its interior.
